About Me

I'm a software developer and consultant from Toronto, Canada, and
I help small businesses automate and streamline their vital processes.


Production tracking is a vital business process for CG and VFX studios. There's a number of solutions for production tracking, but many studios don't use them effectively, nor do they realize the kinds of reporting they could be doing.

I bring a passion for data analysis to my work. I can help you look at your tracking and reporting, and dig through the data to find new ways to look at the status of your production. The better equipped you are with factual, real-time information, the more confident you can be discussing the project with clients and executives alike.

I also bring that same skillset to other industries. If you own a small business that's growing rapidly, your existing solutions for tracking and managing information may not scale well. Once I understand your business at a fundamental level, I can implement database and workflow tracking solutions that will grow with your business and allow you and your employees to focus on your revenue-generating activities. My solutions also incorporate real-time reporting and data analytics to provide you valuable insights into how your business is doing.

CG/VFX
Arc Productions

Arc Productions

Project Info

  • Client Arc Productions
  • Date 2008-2012
  • In CG/VFX
  • Place Toronto, Canada

Project Description

When I arrived at Arc Productions (Starz Animation at the time), they had a very utilitarian production tracker, and all of their HR processes were done on paper.

I spent four years with them building a brand new production tracker from the ground up that was tailor-made to their specific production processes. The software that I built for them managed every aspect of production management: artist assignments, statuses of shots and assets as they moved through the pipe, and even a client-facing review tool that allowed clients to view clips, draw on frames, and submit their feedback. It also provided valuable real-time reporting in both web-based printouts and interactive Excel documents. This software was vital to the success of both CG and VFX projects like Gnomeo & Juliet, Hoodwinked 2, Dolphin Tale and Camelot.

In addition to the production tracker, I also helped to centralize the studio's documentation, and I automated the management of vacation requests.

The documentation system provided a central location for studio documentation, which allowed new hires to get quickly up to speed on studio-specific workflows and tools, HR policies, among other things.

The custom-built vacation management tool allowed artists to request vacation and have that request tracable and visible through the approval chain. It also provided a valuable window for HR to quickly view all of the outstanding and upcoming vacations without needing to track it manually in an Excel spreadsheet or an Outlook calendar.

Project Description

When Dr. Baker first approached me, he had a website that was built by another developer and some great ideas for where he wanted to go with the site. The more I discussed his wishlist and the more I thought about it, the more I realized there was one central thing he was missing: analytics.

Toonbox Entertainment

Toonbox Entertainment

Project Info

  • Client Toonbox Entertainment
  • Date ongoing since 2012
  • In CG/VFX
  • Place Toronto, Ontario, Canada
  • URL www.toonboxent.com

Project Description

Toonbox brought me in to lead the charge on improving their production tracking. Before my arrival, they were mostly using spreadsheets to track production, and each department had their own way of doing things.

After some discussion about bringing in a third party solution or rolling their own proprietary systems, they opted to bring in Shotgun. Once it was up and running, I helped to automate the tracking of assets and shots through the pipe in Shotgun and built real-time reports that queried Shotgun to give producers up-to-the-minute status reports.

In addition to production tracking, I built web-based systems for vacation and timesheet tracking to replace their hard copy counterparts. These systems ensured reporting accuracy for management and provided valuable transparency to employees. I took this system a step further and streamlined their new hire process, providing a central place for HR, Accounting, Systems and Production to track new hires and all the resources that need to be set up for a new employee.

In the Production Technology department, I helped reduce the occurrences of show-stopping toolset release problems by switching them to a new source control system and implementing new toolset development and release processes. We continue to evolve these processes to this day, and I'm excited to continue working with Toonbox and helping them achieve even greater efficiency.
